Calligraphy Artist

I research and study the culture that I want to make art for, and I learn about their symbols, colors, patterns, and values. Then I use calligraphy to write words or phrases that are meaningful or inspirational for that culture, such as greetings, proverbs, or quotes. I also use calligraphy to draw shapes or images that represent the culture, such as flowers, animals, or landmarks. I then transfer my designs to clothes and other items, such as shirts, bags, or mugs, using different techniques, such as printing, painting, or embroidery.

2018-2024

Paints and my & passion in Arts

I love to express my creativity and passion through painting amazing artworks that reflect the culture, hearts, and visions of different people and places. I draw inspiration from the history and arts of various civilizations, such as the ancient Egyptians, the Renaissance, and the modern era. I use different mediums and techniques to capture the essence and beauty of each culture, such as oil, acrylic, watercolor, collage, and graffiti. I also incorporate symbols and motifs that represent the values and emotions of the people, such as the lotus flower, the heart, and the eye.
